Welcome to the culinary heart of Chinatown, where flavors
from across Asia converge in one vibrant hub.

Taste the Flavors of Chinatown

With Chinese (40.9%), Thai (16.2%), Indonesian (7.2%) and Korean (3.6%) as the highest populated cultures in Haymarket, Chinatown offers a truly multicultural dining experience, from authentic street food to modern Asian fusion. Dive in and explore the many tastes that make Haymarket a culinary destination, celebrating the flavors of home for so many.
